INSTRUCTIONS


  • The run is divided into two categories. One is of 5km (Fun Run) and another is of 11 km (Quarter Marathon).
  • Every runner must wear the given T-shirt with chest number on it.
  • The run will start from annapurna hall IIT Ropar and go upto Ropar head. For detailed route click here
  • There are two-three refreshment booths on the route where some fruits and water will be available.
  • Every runner will get breakfast after the run.
  • Our volunteers will supervise the event at every point of the route.
  • If you break any rules at any point of time then you may be disqualified from the run. And any serious action may be taken if needed.
  • There will be volunteers on cycles with medical kit with them.
  • We recommend you to enjoy the route as much as possible by jogging and walking.
  • Collect your kit (chest number and t-shirt) from the Utility Block between 4-8 PM on 1st October.


Event Info

  • Run will start at 6:00 AM (11 KM) and 6:30 AM (5 KM) sharp on 2nd October 2022.
  • Early bird fee for students (until 13/10/2022) - INR 300
  • Registration fee for Faculty and Staff - INR 400
  • Late registration fee (until 18/10/2022) - INR 500
